We provide accessible and high-quality gardening tools and
plant care essentials because we want you and your green
companions to thrive together.

We set ourselves from apart of competitions through
three pillars:

innovation, education, aesthetics

Innovation

Be it our cylinder packaging, self-watering
pots, the online ordering experience, or even
our R&D, we take pride in innovation and
making the plant parenting journey easy and
stress-free.

Education

Buying plants can sometimes be overwhelming,
especially in the beginning. Through our blog,
newsletter, and social channels, we’ve made a
conscious effort to educate and inspire customers
in their journey.

Aesthetics

Our plants always enhance the spaces
they occupy. We make concerted efforts
in not only nurturing our plants in the
best way possible, but making them look
good too.

Challenging times make resilient brands

__________________________________________________________________

In 2020, the world changed. As people got confined to
their homes thanks to COVID-19, they rediscovered the
life-affirming value that plants add to one’s life.

That’s when the purpose became crystal clear to me:

to inspire a world where every plant journey is a
personal revolution of inner change and growth.
